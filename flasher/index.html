Flash your ESP32

Install the RoboWallet firmware directly from your browser — no Arduino IDE, no toolchain. Connect an ESP32-C3 over USB and click below.

Your browser doesn't support WebSerial. Use desktop Chrome, Edge, or Opera.

After flashing
  1. The device prints its Solana address over serial — fund it with devnet SOL for fees.
  2. Run the gateway near the device: node scripts/roborelay.js
  3. Configure Wi-Fi and the gateway address, then watch it pay on-chain.

Full guide: robowallet-core.vercel.app/docs
